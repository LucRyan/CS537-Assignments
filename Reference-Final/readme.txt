------------------------------------------------------------------------
Control:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Car: 	
------------------------------------------------------------------------
	F   	- get off(automatically get off by reaching the area)
   	V  	- switch camera
   	Up	- move forward
	Down 	- move backward
	Right	- turn right
	Left	- turn left
	Q	- quit
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Fly:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Z	- roll
	X	- pitch
	C	- yaw
	Up	- move forward
	Down 	- move backward
	Right	- move right
	Left	- move left
	F2	- turn off light in room
	Q	- quit
------------------------------------------------------------------------
There is a room in the left part of the castle.
